Our Story

GERIS Agri-solutions started in 2021 during the Canada-Africa Innovation fellowship a program of the Engineers Without Borders Canada. Through the onboard training by United Social Venture with an aim of developing sustainable & scalable solution, we were teamed up and drew an inspiration from the SDG No.2 of the UN which focuses on ending global hunger. Having realized that the leading cause of hunger is not absence of enough food but the post harvest handling of agriculture produces, we decided on creation of a product that can be used in control of postharvest wastage of agriculture produces. And here we are with our product.
